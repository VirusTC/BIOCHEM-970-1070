# Facility Engineering: Food-Grade and Medical Storage Standards

This document establishes the facility requirements for hospitals storing the SFWB product before clinical use, satisfying *Food-Grade Refrigeration, Heating, and Storage.pdf*.

## 1. Environmental Control Protocols
*   **Liquid Storage Life:** 21 days when maintained at **2°C to 4°C** in a dedicated, medical-grade refrigerator.
*   **Lyophilized Storage Life:** 24 months when stored at **-20°C** in an ultra-low temperature freezer matrix.
*   **Lighting Control:** Storage bays must be kept completely dark or equipped with yellow/amber UV-blocking filters. Exposure to light wavelengths between 400nm and 500nm will trigger photo-oxidation of the VerduraRX carotene complex, lowering its ability to carry oxygen.

## 2. Automated FDA Audit Interface
To support hourly IRB compassionate care audits, the storage refrigerator controllers must pipe telemetry data directly into the Univac-IX operating system. If a network disruption lasts more than 15 minutes, the on-duty blood bank supervisor will receive an automated alert to perform a manual temperature verification.
