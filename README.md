# Biochem 1070: Medical Lectures, Clinical Protocols & Operational Manuals

Welcome to the archive repository for **Biochem 1070**. This repository serves as a centralized, open-access knowledge base hosting specialized medical lectures, advanced clinical workflows, surgical oncology manuals, and operational guidelines [1].

## Repository Overview

This archive bridges biochemistry, advanced surgical oncological procedures, and healthcare facilities infrastructure. The documentation is organized into clear educational tracks [1]:

### 1. Surgical Oncology & Clinical Protocols
* **Laser Biopsy Manual:** Guidelines for precision surgical margins, tissue ablation, and clinical execution [1].
* **Specimen Handling Checklist:** Protocol steps ensuring cellular integrity of laser-excised diagnostic specimens [1].
* **Oncology Lectures:** Foundational overviews covering the pathophysiological tracking, replication mechanics, and research paradigms surrounding oncogenic viruses ("The Pursuit of Cancer Viruses") [1].

### 2. Advanced Biochemistry & Virology (VirusTC)
* **Protein Cross-Linking:** High-density analysis covering the therapeutic targeting of unstable protein structures [1].
* **Clinical Virology Guides:** Actionable operational handbooks for medical personnel managing the integration and administration of localized healthcare platforms (HuesOS) [1].
* **Clinical Physiology:** Foundational review material tracking hydration dynamics and fluid-electrolyte balance in acute clinical environments [1].

### 3. Facilities Management & Healthcare Operations
* **Industrial & Environmental Controls:** Lectures breaking down food-grade thermal parameters, advanced hospital refrigeration, and sterile medical heating systems [1].
* **Dental Laser Ablation:** Foundational shifts exploring tissue vaporization as a non-invasive scalpel alternative [1].
